Quick Tips

Skincare habits that actually make a difference.

🕐

Be Consistent

Skincare works through consistency, not miracles. Most products need 4–12 weeks of regular use before showing noticeable results. Stick with it.

💤

Sleep is Skincare

Your skin repairs itself during sleep. Aim for 7–9 hours, sleep on a clean pillowcase, and change it weekly to prevent bacteria buildup.

🥤

Hydrate from Within

Drinking water supports overall skin health. While topical products help, internal hydration contributes to a plumper, healthier complexion.

🌡️

Use Lukewarm Water

Hot water can strip natural oils and cause redness. Cold water doesn't dissolve oils effectively. Lukewarm is the sweet spot for cleansing.

🌿

Patch Test New Products

Always patch test a new product on your inner arm or behind your ear for 24–48 hours before applying it to your face.

📱

Clean Your Devices

Your phone screen and hands transfer bacteria to your face. Wipe your phone screen daily and avoid resting your cheek against it.

Purging happens when an active ingredient (like retinol or an AHA/BHA) speeds up cell turnover, temporarily bringing congestion to the surface faster than normal. It typically lasts 4–6 weeks and breakouts appear in areas where you'd normally break out. If breakouts appear in new areas or last longer than 6 weeks, that's likely a reaction, not purging — stop the product.
