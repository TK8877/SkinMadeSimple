Skincare Basics

Build a solid foundation with the essential steps every beginner needs to know.

The Core Four

The fundamental steps of any great skincare routine.

You don't need 12 steps or a $300 serum. Master these four pillars first, and your skin will thank you.

🧼
Step 01

Cleanse

Remove dirt, oil, sunscreen, and makeup. Use a gentle cleanser twice daily — morning and night. Avoid harsh soaps that strip the skin's natural oils.

🌸
Step 02

Tone (Optional)

Balance your skin's pH after cleansing. A good toner can remove remaining traces of cleanser and prepare your skin for serums and moisturizers.

💧
Step 03

Moisturize

Every skin type needs moisture — yes, even oily skin. Hydration helps maintain the skin barrier and prevents overproduction of oil.

☀️
Step 04

SPF (AM Only)

Sunscreen is the single most effective anti-aging product you can use. Apply SPF 30+ every morning — even on cloudy days and when working indoors.

Layering Order

How to layer your products correctly.

The golden rule: apply products from thinnest to thickest. This ensures each product absorbs properly and doesn't block lighter ones.

1️⃣

Cleanser

Always the first step.

2️⃣

Toner

Water-based, very light.

3️⃣

Essence

Lightweight hydration boost.

4️⃣

Serum

Active ingredients, targeted treatments.

5️⃣

Moisturizer

Seals in hydration.

6️⃣

SPF

Last step in AM. Never skip.

Pro Tips

Beginner mistakes to avoid.

⚠️

Over-cleansing

Washing your face more than twice a day can strip natural oils, leading to irritation and rebound oiliness. Stick to twice daily.

🔬

Introducing too many products at once

Add one new product at a time and wait 1–2 weeks before adding another. This way, you can identify what causes reactions.

😴

Skipping sunscreen

UV damage accounts for up to 90% of visible aging signs. Sunscreen is non-negotiable for long-term skin health.

💆

Touching your face

Your hands carry bacteria and oils that can clog pores. Try to avoid touching your face throughout the day.
